About Us

Pandey Traders (Sukhdev Pandey Khad Beej Bhandar) was established in 2002 in Gonda, Uttar Pradesh. Our aim is to provide high-quality agricultural seeds, fertilizers, equipment, and expert guidance to help farmers improve their productivity and income.

Our services include certified seeds for wheat, paddy, mustard, maize, etc., fertilizers like urea and DAP, as well as sales and repair of various agricultural tools. We are also wholesale dealers for leading oil and fertilizer companies, ensuring trust and quality for our customers.
