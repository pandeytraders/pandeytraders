About Us

Founded in 2002 in Gonda, Uttar Pradesh, Pandey Traders (Sukhdev Pandey Khad Beej Bhandar) is committed to empowering local farmers through high-quality seeds, fertilizers, equipment, and expert advice.

Our services include certified seeds (wheat, paddy, mustard, maize), fertilizers like urea and DAP, agricultural equipment sales and repair, and bulk dealership of reputed agri and oil companies.

We believe that farmers need not just products but a strong support network. With 20+ years of experience and a dedicated team, we help farmers at every step.

Our mission is to strengthen the rural agri-system and empower every farmer for a better livelihood and sustainable growth.

We see our business as a partnership with farmers, not just a transaction.
